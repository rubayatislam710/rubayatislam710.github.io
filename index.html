Who I Am

About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka. My academic journey has been driven by a deep curiosity for materials science, sustainable manufacturing, and industrial systems.

My undergraduate research focused on fabricating hybrid polymer composites using recycled LDPE as a matrix, reinforced with natural fibers — an area I am eager to pursue further at the postgraduate level, particularly in Germany.

Beyond engineering, I am a drone pilot and videographer, capturing Bangladesh from perspectives rarely seen from the ground.
