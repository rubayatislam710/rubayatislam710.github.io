ABOUT ME

Mechanical Engineering undergraduate with a strong interest in power generation, heavy manufacturing industries, and large-scale mechanical systems.

Through industrial training and site visits, I have developed a practical understanding of power plants, manufacturing workflows, utility systems, and mechanical equipment used in real industrial environments.

I have closely observed thermal power plant operations — including turbines, boilers, and control room activities — which inspired my career goal to work in power plants or the global energy sector.

My undergraduate research focused on hybrid polymer composites using recycled LDPE reinforced with natural fibers, an area I am eager to pursue further at the postgraduate level, particularly in Germany.

Beyond engineering, I am a drone pilot and aerial videographer, capturing Bangladesh through my YouTube channel, Eagle's Eyes.
